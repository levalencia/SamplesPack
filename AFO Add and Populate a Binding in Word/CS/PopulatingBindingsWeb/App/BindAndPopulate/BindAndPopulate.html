Add and Populate a Binding

Use the first button to add a named content control (with hidden container) and bind to it.

Click one of the Insert buttons to populate the binding with sample content. You can click one to populate the binding and then click the other to replace the bound content.

Note: Add other content to the document or move your insertion point to another position in the body of the document as desired. The bound location will populate regardless of your cursor position.
